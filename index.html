Food Access and Obesity in Philadelphia

Map of Philadelphia Districts and Their % Levels of Obesity


Problem:

Philadelphia’s obesity rate is ranked 9th in the nation and by displaying the disparities between neighborhoods and their access to fresh food we hope to address the importance and need for access to healthier options within lower-income neighborhoods.

[Directions for interactivity]

Impact on Community:

As a result of not having access to places that sell and provide healthy and nutritious food within close distance to their neighborhoods, the corner stores and small food markets that residents are able to easily access sell food that is inexpensive, however, extremely unhealthy. Implementing fresh, accessible and affordable food options within lower-income neighborhoods across Philadelphia ranking 9th in the nation regarding obesity will hopefully begin to drop.
